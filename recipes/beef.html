Beef Stew

ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cubed
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 3 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, brown the beef over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the beef to the slow cooker, along with the potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, and garlic.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the beef broth, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the mixture over the beef and vegetables in the slow cooker.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours, or on high for 4-6 hours.
  6. Serve hot with crusty bread or over rice.
